Paediatric Orthopaedics

The hospital has a long tradition of treating children. Not every orthopaedic problem requires surgery and careful and early management of many paediatric disorders can prevent later deformities

  • Adolescent Hip Pain
  • Elbow (supracondylar) fractures
  • Wrist fractures
  • Forearm fractures
  • Foot and ankle fractures
  • Hip and Pelvic fractures
